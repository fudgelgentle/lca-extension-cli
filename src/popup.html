LCA-Viz

How it works:

  • Activate the Extension: Make sure the LCA-Viz extension is turned on.
  • Automatic Analysis: As you browse, LCA-Viz will scan the web page you're on and show you any relevant carbon emissions data.

What you will see:

LCA-Viz will display information on the carbon emissions for:
  • Electronic devices
  • Manufacturing materials
  • Virtual servers
  • Package shipping
